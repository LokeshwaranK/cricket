On this day 9 years ago, India defeated Pakistan to enter finals of 2011 WC

The Second semi-final of the ICC Cricket World Cup 2011 was played between the Indian cricket team and the Pakistan cricket team at the Punjab Cricket Association Stadium, Mohali on 30 March 2011. India won the toss and elected to bat, making 260 runs in their 50 overs while losing 9 wickets. In second innings Pakistan tend to chase the target and after starting well, fell short; they were all out for 231 runs in 49.5 overs. Thus, India won the match by 29 runs and qualified for the 2011 Cricket World Cup Final, while eliminating Pakistan from the tournament.
